5 Step Interview Process
Put the time in to find the right people upfront.
Follow these steps in order...
  • Introductions
  • Small Talk
  • Information Gathering
  • Questions/ Answers
  • Wrapping Up



Sample Questions
General Questions Leadership Questions Success Questions  
Tell me about yourself Tell me when you took initative.  How do you define success?
Why do you want this job?  Your management style? Tell me about a goal you set and how you achieved it. 
Why are you looking for a newjob? What leadership skills are the most helpful? Your greatest achievement?
