5 Steps to checking references    
Put the time in to find the right people upfront.
Follow these steps in order...
  • Notify candidates at the start
  • Get consent 
  • Be intentional 
  • Let references know their answers are confidential
  • Avoid questions about candidates personal life   



Sample Questions
What was your relationship with the candidate? Tell me about you two worked together. What skills does this candidate have that will help them succeed in this position, and have you seen the candidate perform a similar role? Chat is one of this candidate’s accomplishments that stands out from when you worked with them?  
Can you name two or three of this candidate’s strengths? What are some areas for growth for this candidate? How would you describe the candidate’s working style?
Why did this candidate leave your company? Would you recommend this candidate for the role? Why or why not? something else here
