Types of Interviews
Telephone Prescreening Direct on-on-one Group - 2 types   
Assess the applicant's general communication skills. Describe a situation in which you were able to use persuasion to successfully convince someone to see things your way. here Candidate Group interview - A group of candidates in the same room. Asked the same questions and participate in the same activities     
Clarify unclear items on the applicant's resume. Describe a time when you were faced with a stressful situation that demonstrated your coping skills. Panel Group - A small group of people involved in the hiring process, team members, managers, etc who interview 1 candidate   
Ask about frequent job changes or gaps in employment. Give me a specific example of a time when you used good judgment and logic in solving a problem.
