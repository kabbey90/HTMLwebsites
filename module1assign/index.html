Human Design 101

Welcome to Human Design 101. If you're here, you're probably pretty new to human design, and wondering how you'll ever figure out what all this stuff in your chart means. Don't worry, this course will help introduce you to your bodygraph (don't worry if you don't have one yet, we'll walk you through how to do that, too).

Here's what you can expect to learn:

  • Generate their BodyGraph based on their exact birth date, time, and location using the provided website.
  • Correctly define and discuss their Type, Authority, and Profile with their peers using the provided collaboration tools provided.
  • Summarize how they have been conditioned in a 500-word assignment.
  • Recall the difference between open and defined centers with 80% accuracy on a quiz-style assessment.
  • Recall the difference between red and black gates and channels with 80% accuracy on a quiz-style assessment.
  • Describe the ways in which they are influenced by external energies in a discussion with their peers using the provided collaboration tools provided.
  • Explain the ways in which they are influenced by their design in a discussion with their peers using the provided collaboration tools provided.
  • Correctly identify the Four Transformation Types using Jenna Zoe's calculator.
  • Create a detailed plan to decondition and start living by their design using the SMART method.
  • Explain how the planets impact the interpretation of their defined gates with 80% accuracy on a quiz-style assessment.
  • Compare and contrast the different types of relationship connections with 80% accuracy on a quiz-style assessment.
  • Discuss the common resource places in a discussion with their peers using the provided collaboration tools provided
