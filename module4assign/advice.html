Leadership Advice

“Managers can achieve compliance from their employees, which is merely getting people to do what they’re supposed to do. But when managers lead their people to truly commit to their work, they get them to do MORE because they want to, not because they have to. Commitment = engagement." - Dawn Motsiff



17 of the best advice

  1. Form your own opinions.
  2. Seek commitment, not compliance.
  3. Get out of your office.
  4. Make decisions with your people in mind.
  5. Celebrate Success.
  6. Do the right thing.
  7. Inspire greatness
  8. Align passion with purpose.
  9. Embrace discomfort.
  10. Be real.
  11. Get to know your people.
  12. Influence with your vision.
  13. Know when change is needed.
  14. Be honest.
  15. Show Gratitude.
