Leadership Training

Congratulations! You made it to Unit 3! Here's where it might get a little harder. During this unit, you will learn about leadership techniques but before that read below to get a basic idea of certain concepts you will find are discussed throughout this unit.

You might be wondering "why do I even need leadership training? I've had no problem getting my job done and making sure the budtenders are managed". 

Let's start with defining what a leader. Leaders - In general, the role of a leader is to coach, guide, and inspire others. They motivate teams through challenging times and guide individuals through their career progressions. A leader manages individuals to keep teams aligned and working toward shared goals. They foster a collaborative culture and lead by example Do you see how that might be helpful? If not, by the end of this unit, you will!
