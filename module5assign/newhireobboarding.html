New Hire Onboarding

Welcome to your first Green Spring training! This course is split into 2 units to prepare you for upselling products sold to your patients. This not only helps the store make more money (and hopefully you too), but makes you more of an asset to the Cannabis Industry and prepares you for the next steps in your career whether that be a leadership position, management, sales, consultant, or even a dispensary owner yourself. This is the foundation you need to move further in the direction you choose. The first unit is all about the products you sell in this industry; the prominent product categories, the most prominent cannabinoids, and what terpenes are. Each lesson in this unit will be short and sweet, assuming you already know a lot of the basics about the products, but providing a little more in-depth knowledge to ensure you're really prepared for the second unit.


Lesson 1: Flower

In the first lesson of this module, you will learn about the product type flower. A lot of this information you will likely already know, but pay close attention to discovering something unknown about this product type, or how using it may affect your patients. At the end of the lesson, ask yourself, "Is this something I should be upselling to all of my patients? Why or why not?" and imagine a new patient comes in with no experience with cannabis what so ever, how would you describe this product category to them? What would entice them to choose this product and what should they know in advance? Click the link below to access the lesson content in another tab.
